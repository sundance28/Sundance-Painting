Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 05, 2026

At Sundance Painting (accessible at sundance28.github.io/Sundance-Painting), your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or interact with our advertisements on Facebook (Meta Platforms).

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information:

  • Automatically collected data: When you visit our site, we (and third parties like Meta) may collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, and time spent on the site. This is done via cookies and similar technologies, including the Meta Pixel.
  • Meta Pixel and Facebook Ads: We use the Meta Pixel to understand how users interact with our site after seeing or clicking our Facebook ads. This helps us measure ad effectiveness and show relevant ads. Meta may link this data to your Facebook account if you're logged in.
  • No direct personal collection: We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or other personal details.

How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Improve our website and user experience.
  • Run and optimize Facebook/Meta advertising campaigns (e.g., retargeting visitors with relevant ads).
  • Comply with legal requirements.

Sharing and Selling of Information

We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With Meta Platforms, Inc. (Facebook) as necessary for advertising and analytics via the Meta Pixel (Meta processes this data under their own privacy policy).
  • With service providers who assist us (e.g., web hosting), bound by confidentiality.
  • When required by law (e.g., court order).

We never share or sell data for marketing purposes outside of Meta's advertising ecosystem.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We and Meta use cookies and pixels for functionality and ads. You can manage cookies in your browser settings or opt out of personalized ads via Meta's settings: Facebook Ad Preferences.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us at sundancepainting@outlook.com to exercise these. For Meta-related data, visit Meta's Privacy Center: Meta Privacy Policy.

If you're in California (CCPA/CPRA), we do not "sell" or "share" personal information as defined by law.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information, but no method is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with the updated date.

Contact Us

If you have questions, email us at sundancepainting@outlook.com.
